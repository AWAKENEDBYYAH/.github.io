About

AWAKENED BY YAH MUSIC

Short Bio

AWAKENED BY YAH MUSIC is a truth-based soul and R&B artist creating music rooted in endurance, obedience, and calling on the Name of YAH. Through emotionally rich sound and purpose-driven lyrics, the music speaks to the awakening of Yasharal and those walking a disciplined, set-apart path in a noisy world.

Long Bio

AWAKENED BY YAH MUSIC is a truth-based soul and R&B artist whose music is rooted in endurance, awakening, and alignment with the Word. Each song is crafted to strengthen the Remnant — those seeking truth beyond tradition and walking a disciplined, set-apart path. Blending emotional depth with scripture-anchored themes, AWAKENED BY YAH MUSIC delivers sound that ministers, challenges, and restores.

The mission is not fame, but faithfulness — using music as a tool of remembrance, encouragement, and truth in a generation surrounded by noise.
